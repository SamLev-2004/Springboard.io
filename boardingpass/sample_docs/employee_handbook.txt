ACME Corp Employee Handbook
VACATION POLICY
All full-time employees receive 15 days paid vacation annually, accrued at 1.25 days per month.
Vacation requests must be submitted 2 weeks in advance via the HR portal at hr.acmecorp.com.
Unused vacation up to 5 days rolls over to the following year.
BENEFITS
Health and dental insurance begins on your first day of employment.
Vision insurance begins after 90 days.
RRSP matching: ACME matches up to 4% of your annual salary. Enroll via benefits.acmecorp.com.
Employee wellness budget: $500/year for gym memberships, mental health apps, or ergonomic equipment.
EXPENSE POLICY
All work expenses must be submitted within 30 days via Expensify.
Expenses over $500 require manager pre-approval before purchase.
Receipts required for all amounts over $25.
Software subscriptions must be approved by your department head before purchase.
REMOTE WORK
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
Full remote is available for roles designated as remote-eligible. Check your offer letter.
Core collaboration hours are 10am-3pm in your local timezone.
CODE OF CONDUCT
Treat all colleagues with respect regardless of role or seniority.
Discrimination or harassment of any kind will result in immediate review.
Raise concerns with your manager or confidentially at ethics@acmecorp.com.
PERFORMANCE REVIEWS
Reviews occur every 6 months in June and December.
New hires receive a 30-day, 60-day, and 90-day check-in with their manager.
Promotion decisions are made annually in December based on review outcomes.
