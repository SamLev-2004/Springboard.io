ACME Corp IT Onboarding Guide
LAPTOP SETUP
Your laptop will be staged and ready on your first day at your desk or shipped to your home.
Contact it@acmecorp.com or post in Slack #it-support for any setup issues.
Standard setup issues are resolved within 1 business day.
For urgent issues on day 1: call the IT hotline at ext. 5000.
SOFTWARE ACCESS
You will receive email invitations to Slack, Notion, and Google Workspace within 24 hours of your start date.
For additional software: submit a request at helpdesk.acmecorp.com — approval takes 1-3 business days.
Licensed software (Adobe, Figma, etc.) requires department head approval before IT can provision.
VPN
Download Cisco AnyConnect from software.acmecorp.com/vpn.
Your VPN credentials are sent to your personal email the business day before your start date.
VPN is required for accessing internal systems outside the office.
PASSWORD POLICY
Passwords must be at least 12 characters, include a number and a symbol.
Passwords must be changed every 90 days — you will receive a reminder 7 days before expiry.
Use the company 1Password account for all credential management. Never store passwords in plain text.
SECURITY
Never share your credentials with anyone, including IT staff (we will never ask).
Report suspicious emails to security@acmecorp.com immediately.
Lock your screen (Win+L or Cmd+Ctrl+Q) whenever you leave your desk.
